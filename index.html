How Spotify Tracks Compare

This short story explores 5,000 Spotify songs, trimmed to remove outliers. We examine whether longer songs, more words in the title, or faster tempos align with higher popularity scores.

The scatter plots show that none of these factors strongly predict popularity. Duration shows virtually no correlation, while track name length trends slightly downward. Faster tempos seem to lift ratings just a bit.
